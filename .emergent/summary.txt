The Loop mobile marketplace is an anti-waste platform for iOS and Android, focusing on food donations and paid non-food item sales in French, with a modern, eco-friendly design. Core features include email/password authentication, Stripe Connect for escrow payments with QR/code handoff, and MapLibre/OpenStreetMap for location (later transitioned to Leaflet via WebView). Users can post food (with urgency) or market items (with pricing/offer), view details, chat, checkout, and manage handoffs/disputes. Recent product requirements expanded to include:

1.  **Stores Feature**:
    *   **View**: Map/list toggle for partner stores, list by default.
    *   **Filters**: Distance, category (Grocery, GMS, Recyclerie, Repair), Open Now, With Deals, Followed.
    *   **Sorting**: Distance (default), number of deals, rating (V2).
    *   **Store Detail Page**: Name, logo, clickable address, hours, contact, website, description, partner badge.
    *   **Deals/Offers**: Stores can post promotions (MVP via simple back-office/CSV; dashboard V2). Deals include title, description, discount type, prices, expiration, quantity, category, store ID. No donations, only anti-waste promos, auto-expire.
    *   **Notifications**: Push notifications to followers and nearby users (opt-in), with anti-spam.
    *   **Partner Signup Form**: Accessible from Stores tab, includes legal name, business name, address (geocoded), type, contact, hours, description, URL, estimated anti-waste volume. Stored in DB with internal email notification.
    *   **Tab**: Re-activate the Stores tab as the 3rd tab.
2.  **Barcode Scanning**: For food donations, to auto-fill item details (title, description, nutritional info, allergens, DDM info) via OpenFoodFacts API.
3.  **Offer Negotiation Enhancements**:
    *   When a buyer makes an offer, a message with the proposal should appear in the seller's chat with Accept and Refuse buttons.
    *   When a seller makes a counter-offer, Accept and Refuse buttons should appear for the buyer in the chat.
    *   After an offer is accepted, the buyer should see a Pay Now button to proceed with the payment, along with a timer indicating when the item will be unlocked if not paid.
